I also adjusted the cyan colors to make them softer and increased the blue-red hue such that the brick building and sky would stand out. Since I wanted the building and sky to stand out, I made sure not to adjusted the yellow-green hue so that the school building that is reflected in the window doesn't really pop out unless you are focused on the image. I wanted the scenery to draw viewers attention + make them smile.
